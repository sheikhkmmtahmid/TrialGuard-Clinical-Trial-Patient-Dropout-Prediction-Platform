Workflow

How TrialGuard Works

01

Ingest & Engineer

Upload patient demographics and visit data via CSV or REST API. The pipeline engineers 25+ predictive features: visit frequency trends, adverse event rates, adherence trajectories, and distance risk flags.

02

Score & Stratify

XGBoost assigns a dropout probability (0–1) at every visit. Cox PH estimates the individual dropout timeline. Patients are stratified into Low / Medium / High / Critical tiers with colour-coded alerts.

03

Intervene & Retain

Coordinators receive a 60-day dashboard with SHAP-driven explanations and plain-English risk summaries. Log interventions, download PDF reports, and track retention outcomes — before the patient is lost.
